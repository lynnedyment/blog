Riku's Special Art... and Our Little Helper

IM'ing with Bryn today:

Lynne says:
Riku napping... 10:30 a.m. on the dot

Lynne says:
says Riku "I'm going to do art now. Special art. No touching. I'm working now"
Lynne says:
and he is indeed busy doing art
Lynne says:
"no touching the art. Special art. No touching, okay?"
bryn says:
wow!
Lynne says:
he just keeps saying over and over... "making special art. No touching, okay?"
Lynne says:
it's very cute...
bryn says:
he was drawing this morning, and didn't want me to draw on the sheet
Lynne says:
now saying "What shall we make? Maybe you would like a hat? Maybe you would like this art?"
bryn says:
usually we just draw together
Lynne says:
He is going on and on... typing away to try to get it all
bryn says:
adorable
bryn says:
first time he's said "making art"
Lynne says:
"Maybe draw a circle?"
Lynne says:
first time i have heard this too
bryn says:
like his overalls?
Lynne says:
oh yes, but I like overalls a lot... he is asking me to come and work on his little chair
Lynne says:
Riku LOVES his little table (we bought it for him at IKEA recently)
bryn says:
that's awesome

OK.. here is a another new one for me... in the car coming home after dropping off Akane, Riku was chattering away to himself and making rolling motions with his hands... very busy
Lynne says:
and then he said to me "Riku speaking Japanese"!
Lynne says:
I repeated it to him, asking "You are speaking Japanese?" and he said "yes, Riku speaking Japanese".
bryn says:
yeah, we kind of kid about that...
bryn says:
if he's talking gibberish, i'll sometimes say "are you speaking japanese?"

later still...

Lynne says:
I'm fading fast here... off I go to tidy Riku's room. Today he helped me fold the two blankets he uses during his naps... he took one side and I took the other... he likes his room tidy.. interesting, but then so did you
bryn says:
he helped carry stuff up from the car
Lynne says:
Karyn said today that Riku looks so much like you! She thought it was his smile perhaps. Akane says more people are saying that too, although I see many of Akane's mannerisms in him lately
bryn says:
he carried the banana bread up.... wanted to participate
Lynne says:
he is a helper for sure...
Lynne says:
yes, that's what it is... he pulls up the chair to help cook, and today helped load up a bag with things for the baby!
Lynne says:
he is such a special little boy
bryn says:
i'm glad you think so!
Lynne says:
yeah, me and the universe!
