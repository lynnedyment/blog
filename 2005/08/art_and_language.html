Art and Language

riku napping... 10:30 a.m. on the dot
Lynne says:
says Riku "I'm going to do art now. Special art. No touching. I'm working now"
Lynne says:
and he is indeed busy doing art
Lynne says:
"no touching the art. Special art. No touching, okay?"
bryn says:
wow!
Lynne says:
he just keeps saying over and over... "making special art. No touching, okay?"
Lynne says:
it's very cute...
bryn says:
he was drawing this morning, and didn't want me to draw on the sheet
Lynne says:
now saying "What shall we make? Maybe you would like a hat? Maybe you would like this art?"
bryn says:
usually we just draw together
Lynne says:
He is going on and on... typing away to try to get it all
bryn says:
adorable
bryn says:
first time he's said "making art"
Lynne says:
"Maybe draw a circle?"
Lynne says:
first time i have heard this too
bryn says:
like his overalls?
Lynne says:
oh yes, but I like overalls a lot... he is asking me to come and work on his little chair
Lynne says:
Riku LOVES his little table
bryn says:
that's awesome

OK.. here is a new one for me too... in the car coming home after dropping off Akane, Riku was chattering away to himself and making rolling motions with his hands... very busy
Lynne says:
and then he said to me "Riku speaking Japanese"!
Lynne says:
I repeated it to him, saying Yyou are speaking Japanese?" and he said "yes, Riku speaking Japanese".
bryn says:
yeah, we kind of kid about that...
bryn says:
if he's talking gibberish, i'll sometimes say "are you speaking japanese?"
