At Home with Amelia

Back to the laundry today; lots to do, but it helped knowing that Amelia would join me at home today. Susan had some errands in this part of town to run, so she dropped Amelia off for me, about 12:15. We spent about forty-five minutes just hanging about with Amelia playing in the living room with the chickens, in the kitchen pulling out the plastics from the drawer, rediscovering the basket of toys in the kitchen, and "cruising" whilst holding on to the walls, sofa, and chairs.

After that we headed out for a very long walk through the Mission District. Amelia sat up so nicely in our stroller and enjoyed eating snacks from the little tray (raisins and biscuits. After that, Amelia was asleep almost instantly and slept for a full two hours, enjoying more snacks and water when she woke up. I took advantage of her well- rested and well-fed little self and did some videotaping of her. What a wonderful age this is!

After such a late night last night, I planned on an early night tonight, but stayed up later than anticipated trying to catch up on emails.

Six more days 'til Riku and Akane fly in!
