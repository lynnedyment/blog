Monday... back in the Park

This morning was a busy day, running errands in the morning with Doug, starting the laundry, and ending with a hair cut in Noe Valley. And then it was time to get back to my walking routine with Amelia, and what better place to start than in Golden Gate Park, our old haunt. Amelia was at the door to greet me with her big smiles, standing proudly (she took three steps when I was away!), and happy to see me. The feeling was certainly mutual, and off we went for a stroll to Stow Lake and back again, a two and a half hour trip in all.

Unfortunately Amelia's nap was interrupted by a young boy shouting into her carriage when she was in a deep sleep and that was it, no more naptime for her. Instead, I was treated to lots of finger pointing and connecting time, and best of all, lots of big puckers-up for kisses all the way home. Amelia also waves with great vigour from side to side, which is just adorable. It's good to be home and back on track with long walks. The air smells just wonderful!

Drove Doug to the Oakland airport at 8 p.m for his three day trip to Virginia.

Riku and Akane will be home in just one week... counting down the days now.

4 October 2004
