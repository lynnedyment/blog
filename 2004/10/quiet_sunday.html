Quiet Sunday

Today was a very low-key quiet day for me. Doug and Bryn went off to IKEA and Berkeley while I stayed home and caught up on some paper work and loose ends. When they returned, Doug drove to San Mateo to attend a adventure/travel show, where the bag he helped designed was being introduced, while Bryn and I went to Costco and on a vacuum cleaner search for him. From there we drove to Noe Valley on a search for a baby doll for Amelia, and then on to West Portal when I had no luck either. I settled for a tiny tub doll, not exactly what I wanted, so I will continue the search and make it a Christmas gift.

Bryn stopped for a submarine sandwich for his dinner before dropping me off at home. Doug and I had a quick and easy dinner and watched a few videos before calling it a day.

I spoke with Riku on the phone tonight for a few minutes; he was listening, but unlike the other day I didn't get a giggle out of him this time. I did tell him however that he would be home very soon, just one and a half days to go!
