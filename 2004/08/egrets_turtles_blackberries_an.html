Egrets, Turtles, Blackberries, and... Amelia!

Doug and I walked a sleepy little girl through Golden Gate Park today. It was a lovely late afternoon walk, not too hot and not too cool, but just right! Amelia fell asleep just seven minutes after heading down the Panhandle, and slept past the rose garden, missed the snowy white egret, and didn't get to eat even one wild blackberry from the big patch behind Stow Lake. The big juicy ones are getting harder to find but Doug took a coat hanger with him today to pull down some of the less reachable and fuller branches. Yum! Susan guessed, from the blackberry juice on my cheek, what we had been up to. A day or two more of hot sunny days will ripen up lots more, so I'm hoping for one more fill of them before heading off to Ontario next week.

Oh yes, we saw our first turtle today in the pond, and then spotted three more... very exciting! The snowy egret was just beautiful and stuck around long enough for Doug to take a few photos of him.
