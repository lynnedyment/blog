Deja Vu

I spent the loveliest of days with Amelia today, as she spent much longer playing in the house than ever before. She had napped in the car on the way over so, when Susan left her here, Amelia was ready to move... and move she did.

It was like having Riku here at the same stage, crawling faster than one would imagine possible, hanging on to my leg as I made fruit salad, and walking along a long hallway, side-stepping the entire length of it, just the way her daddy described this new activity to me. Amelia also decided to turn around to slide down on her own today... but from the top of the steps... I was videotaping her and so there is a fast ending to that movie!

I also opened up a big bottom drawer in the kitchen and Amelia discovered as well just how much fun it is to drop cookie cutters all over the floor. Riku still likes to get into this drawer, so this stage will last a little longer yet. The smaller drawer beside it holds plastics (containers, lids, cups, etc.) and is an everlasting source of pleasure.

We left the house at 3 p.m. to meet Akane and Riku for what I hoped would be her long afternoon nap but no such luck; the Mission District is just too busy and interesting for sleeping, so Amelia waited for a quieter street and napped for a half hour only.

At one point she and Riku reached out hands to each other; he smiled and patted her. I hope that one day they will be good buddies.
