Strolling with Amelia

Well, everyone should be so lucky as to be greeted with the big beaming smile that awaited me today! Amelia was very happy to see me and showed me how much in the most delightful way possible. Her whole face lit up, she reached out for me, put those chubby little arms around me and buried her head in my neck. Nothing could be sweeter!

Amelia was ready for a nap, as she and Mommy had been out for a long walk and time in the sandbox earlier in the afternoon. After struggling to get her sunhat off (I was sure she wouldn't succeed as it was under her head... but what do I know?) Amelia gave in and slept for almost two hours.

She awakened slowly, giving me a sweet smile, closing her eyes again for a few minutes and then coming around with an even bigger smile.

I really love my walk out to Stow Lake, and today was a perfect day for it; the sun wasn't shining, so it wasn't too warm, but instead the ideal temperature for strolling through the rose garden, on the way out to the lake and on the way back.
