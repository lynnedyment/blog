Happy Birthday Tay, and a Few Riku Moments Too

Last week, I won tickets to a movie "The Mother", plus a $30 gift certificate towards lunch at a very nice seafood restaurant called "McCormick's and Kuleto's", located at Ghirardelli Square. I had grilled wild King salmon with saffron rice and vegetables, and Doug enjoyed mahi mahi with mashed potatoes and veg'. Yum! We plan to see the movie on Thursday evening.

Off we went to see what I thought was a very very boring Canadian movie; I walked out before my snoring woke up everyone else in the audience!

Today is Tay's birthday and at 8 p.m. we joined Tay, Mike, and their lovely daughter Cody for a most delicious fresh fruit tart and orange chocolate truffles, served up with good conversation and some laughs.

Following that, we stopped in at Hampshire Street so Doug could say "au revoir" to Riku; Doug is off to Houston tomorrow, arriving back home on Thursday morning.

Riku is becoming quite the "Bompa's" boy; he immediately led Doug into his room so they could leaf through the big car book... you know, engines, steering wheels, tires... all that kind of thing. They shared some guy time together while I chatted with Bryn and Akane.

I ended up leaving my early birthday gift from Tay there with Riku, who was busy looking at it as we were saying goodbye... "My First Thousand Words in Japanese". I'm hoping he will help me with it; he's already ahead of me as I believe I now have three Japanese words down pat. I'm a quick study... :)

Akane cut Riku's hair two days ago and I just adore the way it looks, with short bangs and layered so that his waves are showing.
