A Foggy SF Morning

Walked with sweet Amelia in the early morning fog today; her new home is right across the street from the Panhandle so we strolled through the mist, both of us admiring the massive eucalyptus trees, the various pines, and a few palm trees thrown in for some west coast variety. What an odd but wonderful assortment of greenery and the smell of the eucalyptus was intensified by the heavy mist. Amelia decided it was far too fine a day to nap!

4 June 2004
