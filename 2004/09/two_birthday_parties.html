Two Birthday Parties

Bryn's early birthday party was held at Hans' today whilst Caillie's was held at Kelly's and Gregg's, both from 2 p.m.-4:30. This morning, Gregg set up a stage on the back deck for the "Rock Star" event for Caillie, complete with backdrop, a poster, and a white board with the names of the "stars". Backstage was a karaoke machine and lots of cd's. I made chocolate cupcakes, Kelly and I put together the loot tumblers and bowls and they looked great, and all was ready for eight little girls by 2 p.m.

At Kelly's, the girls came in, some got right into the makeup while others chose some special clothes to wear. Meanwhile, at Bryn's party, he was being decorated with whipped cream and toppings, on his chest and face, which the guests dug into after they finished the job! I missed out seeing this but Hans did show me a few photos... weird stuff!!!! :) Bryn was beaming though and obviously enjoyed it all; he loves a party even if he is the "cake"!

The rock show started off a little quietly as the girls took some time to warm up but eventually they did and by the end of the last session most of the girls were on stage as back up singers and dancers. Kelly made a tape and it will be lots of fun to see that next week when I am back in Toronto.

Things were winding down nicely by 4:15 so we got everything cleaned up and organized in time to leave the house at 6 p.m.... me to the bus terminal for a 6:30 bus to Niagara Falls and Kelly and Gregg to a dinner party and club night in Oakville.

Gerry, Lionel, Cheryl, and Kenny were there to pick me up at 8 p.m. Tea and tidbits at Tim Horton's, home to make a couple of phone calls, and off we went to bed at 11 p.m.

18 September 2004
