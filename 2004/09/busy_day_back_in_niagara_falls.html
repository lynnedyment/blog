Busy Day... Back in Niagara Falls

Started off with a busy day and ended with one too. I was up early for a shower before everyone else got up. Downstairs to make hot cereal for the children, tidied up the kitchen and packed a small suitcase for my trip to the Falls. I caught the 9:30 bus, dozed and went through a couple of Prevention magazines before arriving just before 11:30.

Lionel, my old stand-by, was there to drop me off at Beth's. We played a few games of cards and then I left her to get ready for work, while I walked to Uncle Frank's. It was threatening rain, and Lionel said he would pick me up if it did rain, but it held off and I enjoyed a forty minute walk over to Uncle Frank's.

Got lots done in just a few hours... went through a huge amount of clothing, sorted it into boxes, and have it ready to take away tomorrow morning when I will have the car.

Uncle Frank invited me to have dinner with him, Josie, By', and one or two of the girls but instead I walked over to Sheldon Street, hoping to spend a few hours with Gerry and Lionel. The house was empty when I walked in, I left a message on Gerry's cell phone, but soon realized they wouldn't be home for dinner. I had a tomato sandwich, leafed through a magazine, but soon realized I was tired and decided to go to bed early after checking my emails.

Gerry and Lionel walked in just as I was going down to bed; they were out with Cheryl and Kenny, planning a cruise together in October! How exciting for them all!

Slept so well and had a really good night's sleep.

13 September 2004
