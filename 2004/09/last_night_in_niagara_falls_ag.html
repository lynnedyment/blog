Last Night in Niagara Falls... again!

This will be short and sweet as I am too tired to think straight. It was a very busy day, packed every minute with one more little job I wanted to get to... unpacked the car from last night, drove to WalMart to copy two photos of Uncle Harry, bought new jeans, more penny rollers, cards, then drove to Beth's to deliver goodies (which she was delighted with)and squeezed in two games of cards too!, and then had a relaxing lunch at "John's" with Uncle Frank.

Back at his house we emptied his big freezer, and then I puttered around doing a few easy little jobs... made up some of Auntie Rita's rhubarb, another of black currants she picked last summer, and left three dessert dishes of custard and fruit for UF to snack on while I'm gone and more to make up himself. It is such a pleasure to cook for Uncle Frank, especially when I know how much he enjoys these little extra treats.

At 5 p.m. we went to Ian's to drop off the frozen bits... met Lisa, who was charming and friendly, Ian showed Uncle Frank through his new abode, and then we met up with Vicki, Mitchel, and Alanna!, who (along with David, of course) are living just a few doors down from Ian while their new house is being completed. I took a photo of the children, so eventually it will be up in an album. They were very sweet and friendly, even though they don't know me from Adam.

Leaving there we drove to St. Catherines for a shared plate of eggplant parmigiana at "Lena Linguine's". UF liked it very much too!

Had a few minutes chatting with Gerry before she went off to bed... and now I plan to do the same thing, before (once again) it is midnight before my light goes out.

21 September 2004
