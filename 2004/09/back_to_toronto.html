Back to Toronto...

Caught the 11:15 a.m. bus back to Toronto this morning, after spending most of the morning sorting through all the bags, suitcases, etc. in my room, in preparation for leaving in a big hurry in another week and a bit. Oh, also bought some delicious tofu rolls from the Chinese restaurant opposite the bus terminal for my lunch on the bus; their food is delicious and I enjoyed every bite of it.

Once in Toronto, I stored my big bag in a locker and did some birthday shopping for Kelly downtown. Took the subway home in time to meet the children at 3:30 at school. Had some time to chat with Kelly, which was lovely, while the children played outside and/or practiced the piano.

After Bryant left with his mom (Myk' was at a friend's), Bryn and I caught the subway downtown for dinner at Movenpic... cheap date... pizza for him and a shared plate of rosti between us.

Quiet evening at home, watched a little t.v., did a bit of laundry, answered some emails, and read all of Akane's latest blog entries which I couldn't access from Gerry's.

Such a pleasure to see Riku's little face on the blogs. I'm missing both Riku and Amelia a lot this week. Three weeks is a long time away from those ever-changing little ones.

22 September 2004
