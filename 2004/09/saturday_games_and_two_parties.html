Saturday Games and Two Parties

Today started off busy... everyone (all nine of us after a too-late-to-bed for the children) dashing to get out the door for a 10:30 a.m. soccer game start. We had to make two trips to the field as there were more of us than seats in the van. Gregg and I went off with Sam, Sean, Bryn, Myk' and Bry'; I drove back to the house, while Kelly finished showering, and then I packed up enough food to feed us over the course of three games. Caillie joined us on the return trip to the field.

It was a great day; Bryn's team won all three of the games and in between cheering we ate, took photos, and the children played in the playground... lots of photo opportunities today.

Back home with just a little over an hour to take a deep breath, get Caillie ready for a birthday party/sleepover and for the rest of us to head out to Whitby for a party. This was held by a friend of Gregg's, the occasion being his daughter's seventh birthday party. Bry', Myk' and Bryn had a great time in the pool and spent most of the afternoon and early evening in the water. The adults sat around on the deck chatting and eating great food... grilled portobello mushrooms, oven-grilled zucchini with parmesan cheese, huge salad, lots of vegies, and birthday cake.

Gregg's friends are quite outrageous and zany, going to great lengths to embarrass each other for our entertainment. It was fun finally putting faces and personalities to the people I've been hearing about for over two years. I also spoke for some time with a lovely warm woman from Glasgow and exchanged some family history.

Home by 9 p.m. and hustled the children right to bed; Bryn was particularly tired after three games of running around in the heat. The adults too crashed early and we all headed up to bed by 11:30 p.m.

11 September 2004
