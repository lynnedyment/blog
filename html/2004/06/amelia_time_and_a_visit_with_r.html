Amelia Time and a Visit with Riku

This morning our guest, Charles, arrived from Australia; he was with us for two days and they flew by. It was the first time I had met him and after Doug picked him up from his 10 a.m. flight we sat in the living room chatting for a couple of hours. Charles is a real people person and tells some great stories. Gotta love that Australian accent...

Then the day got busy! Susan and Kevin came by to drop some things off in storage while I held Amelia and had a bit of a visit with Susan. Meanwhile, my friend Maxine came by to pick up a few items for a garage sale, met Susan, Kevin, and Amelia for the first time, and introduced Amelia to her dog, Anya. Amelia was totally fascinated, leaning way over my arm to get a better look at Anya on the sidewalk below us; she is obviously her mother's child!

Doug and Charles went off to pick up some groceries and movie tickets for me, so Amelia and I snuck off for a nice long stroller ride thorugh the Mission District... not our usual long one, but enough for a fifty-five minute snooze for Amelia and a quick hello at Riku's house.

Riku may remember when Amelia pulled his hair three months ago so he didn't get too close to her, but she gave Akane a big smile in the kitchen, and them moved on to the living room to charm Uncle Bryn. They were both wearing stripes that day, and so made a very attractive couple; he "flew" her up in the air and down again, and she leaned against his legs, beaming her big smile his way, once again winning his heart.

I had started dinner in the late morning, but that was as far as it got, so Doug suggested dinner out (what a welcome idea that was!) and off we went to Marnee Thai for a most delicious dinner. It has been years since we have been there and it is as good as it always was; Charles liked it very much too and we were all ready for an early night when we got home.

 

19 June 2004
