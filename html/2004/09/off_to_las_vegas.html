Off to Las Vegas!

... Kelly and Gregg that is! They left at 6 a.m. today and just as Kelly predicted I didn't get back to sleep. That was fine though as I had my shower over with early and was all set when the children wakened. It was a busy day, filled with the usual walks to and from school (just Bryn and Caillie home for lunch), some time throwing the ball to Manitou to tire him out for his naps, and some general work around the house.

I did have a long chat with Vicki this morning and then a longer one with Gail tonight. Vicki will be with me tomorrow morning for a visit and Gail sounds just awful; that poor girl is in obvious pain and has been told to take sixteen Tylenols a day for pain. Surgery in three plus weeks which will be a very long time indeed with the pain she is having tonight.

Walked Caillie to piano, while Bryn was at his final soccer try-out; he made the school team! and we are all delighted for him. He plans to try out for badminton as well. No, Bryn Sr., I don't think they have ping pong at the school.

Bryant and Myk' were here after school for a couple of hours; snacks, piano practice, and a trip to the piano teacher's house with Caillie, after which their mom picked them up.

I'm more than ready for bed tonight...

23 September 2004
