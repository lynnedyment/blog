Chaotic Tuesday

But a good chaotic, as my kitchen is in the process of being professionally painted at last! The painters arrived at 8 a.m. and it wasn't long before I had no access to the laundry room, pantry or the back door, with a plastic door blocking me off from the eating area.

All the furniture is in Riku's room, the dining room,and/or the living room, the walls are bare, and three men are buzzing around putting up tape, applying patching material, sanding and cleaning the walls.

I spent most of the day on the computer sorting photos in various albums, took a cup of tea and some fruit upstairs at 4 p.m. when I had the house to myself again, and enjoyed the quiet. By 5 p.m. I was ready for a little fresh air, so called Akane and arranged to spend a little time with Riku before Bryn got home. However, he was there when I arrived a few minutes later (I ran over), and the three of us spent some time building blocks, a tunnel, and reading a bit before Akane called them for dinner. Riku is such fun to be with; he enjoys having company and is a happy little fellow, not be mention handsome as can be!

It was a good end to my day and that evening Gail, Candy, and I were chatting about our upcoming trip to Hawaii (Beth was at work) and before I went to bed, Gail and I had booked our flight from Oakland to Honolulu, for February 16th. Now it's Candy and Beth's turn, and we are hoping that we all end up travelling together.

26 October 2004
