Expecting Company

Busy day today as I was expecting Celeste and her friend Steve in at some point. I did work on a photo album for a couple of hours before deciding that I really did have to clean the bathroom, do a few little jobs in the guest room and a quick vacuuming. I was hoping to get out for a walk with Amelia as it was a gorgeous day, but didn't hear from Celeste until later than I anticipated. I was hoping to spend some time every day this week with her.

Celeste and Steve arrived about 4:30 and we sat and chatted in the living room for quite a while; Celeste was starving but Bryn decided he would like to join us for dinner, so we met him at the Roosevelt Tamale Parlour at 6 p.m.

It has been a long time since Celeste and Bryn have seen each other and we all had a long leisurely and most delicious meal together. Bryn tried my favourite dish there, chili rellenos, and really liked it, and he and Steve shared two plates of the same and cheese and chicken enchiladas. Yum!

Celeste had been anticipating Mitchell's ice cream for dessert, as she used to go to this 1954 ice cream parlour when she was a little girl living in the city. So off we went; as usual the place was busy as it always is, and Bryn had purple yam ice cream while Celeste and Steve both had Mexican chocolate covered with chocolate sauce. I abstained... just too comfortable and happy after my chili relleno.

Back home for a bit of chatting but we were all more than ready for bed at 10:15, after a busy day for everyone.

Five days to go, and counting...
