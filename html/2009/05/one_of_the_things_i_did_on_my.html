One of the Things I Did on Spring Vacation — aka "Stepping Into The Hole"

OK, I have written plenty about having stepped into "the hole", and its aftermaths, so here are a few pics' of that experience.

Mirei near the offending hole. It looks so innocent when covered, doesn't it?

Me crashed out on the sofa the next day, where I stayed put for many many hours over the next eleven days.

Mirei at the playground tending to the scratch on my hand, the injury that worried her the most.

I didn't realize for many days that I had scraped/bruised my entire left leg, from hip to ankle, while sliding into that hole. I had Bryn take a photo of it.

Next is a photo of me on the sofa wearing the wonderful pressure belt/bandage that I was still wearing a month later. I could sit there comfortably for a couple of hours, reading to the children, watching them play, and working on Akane's laptop.

On the day I left Japan, I talked Bryn into stepping into the hole (having refused his offer to lower me in!) to show the depth of it, and then I was off to California, after a final goodbye to my nemesis.
