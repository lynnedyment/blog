Mirei and I Make a Carrot Cake

Mirei was ready to settle in at home after her fun, busy day out with Sean and Gen' at the pumpkin patch. We were down to the last tiny date square that we baked last week, which Mirei finished for her snack, so it was time to get baking. So, with hands washed and her apron on, Mirei lifted the kitchen chair over to the counter and we got started.

Today Mirei figured out how to get her thumbs in each side of the egg, and pull it apart with less mess, and we were both delighted with this new skill! "I did it, I did it, all by myself!"

The photos tell it all (although there are more photos not included in this posting).
