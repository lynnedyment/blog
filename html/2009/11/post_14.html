Home All Day

Doug had decided to go to a double feature at the Balboa Theatre today, and so he did lots of photo work for me in the morning.

I planned to stay home and whip through more emails, work on older blog entries that don't have photos in them, and read through some more magazines. I am making great headway in all of these areas, but decided to clean the laundry room first, and get all of the activity books and games back in place after Mirei's visit.

To my surprise, Doug came walking back in about twenty minutes after he left. He had just missed the bus, and we decided that was best anyway as we had some errands we wanted to run in the car.

That was our day... some time in the house and a little time outside, after a rainy beginning to the day. The sun came out later in the afternoon and I did a little sweepingand tidying up in the back yard just to take advantage of what turned into a gorgeous California November day.

No photos for today, so I shall add in just one of the many beautiful shots of roses I have taken in Golden Gate park over the years.
