The Leader of the Pack

Nice slow morning around here, after my always pleasant walk to school with Riku. He reminded me once more not to meet him right at the crosswalk; he is ready to be more independent for sure. It was threatening rain, but the day was merely overcast.

Bryn was working out of the house at a cafe, and Akane was out after dropping Mirei at school, and so I did a little tidying up in the kitchen and in my room, and then sat emailing and blogging.

My new spot to meet Riku is a mere five minutes away, and so I was right on time to spot Riku leading the group of children today, with a big smile on his face. I took a photo of a group of five children before they all headed off in different directions.

Yuu-kun came over to visit today and the boys were busy for a long time making paper airplanes. They were flying here, there and everywhere and they had such fun with it all.

We had a quiet late afternoon, with piano practice for Riku, and then he and Mirei sat on the sofa while he read to her. It was the cutest thing to see and hear.

The children tubbed together for a long time tonight, and played with ducks, and "babies", and boats. I so enjoy tub time, and always have. We sang our favourite "Swimming, swimming, in the swimming pool..." about five times. Miei particularly gets into the action of it and Riku smiles at her as she does the There was too much splashing going on with that vigourous swimming to take my camera in there!

Riku was asleep so quickly tonight, sprawled out on my bed, and I crept in with my camera to capture this moment.

Akane fell asleep with Mirei, so I had lots of time for emails and blogging tonight.

17 April 2009
