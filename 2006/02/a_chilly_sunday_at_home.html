A Chilly Sunday... at Home

The day started off very early, waking at 4:15 a.m. while Doug did the final packing for his trip to Toronto. I drove him to the airport, leaving at 4:50, and was back in bed at exactly 5:30, nice and cozy with the heated bed buddies tucked in before I left. Surprisingly, I was asleep almost instantly, after reading for about a minute and a half, and slept until 8:30. The house was cold, and I was reluctant to get out to turn on the heat! Brrr!

I had a quiet but productive day at home, busy with various projects... cards, general tidying, and working my way through lots of emails. I am down to just 75, down from 630 three weeks ago. There is finally light at the end of the tunnel.

I took advantage of the quiet house and sat and read through some magazines while drinking the new Trader Joe's chocolate-flavoured tea. Yum! It's subtle and delicious, and a nice change from my usual black tea.

Did a little IMing with Kelly. Doug had arrived, and we exchanged a couple of emails before he went off to have dinner with Kelly, Gregg, Myk', and Bryant. They leave for Mexico on Wednesday, so he won't be seeing them after tomorrow.

Seemed very quiet around here today without a child's voice even once in my day.

Up to bed to read at 10:40, bed buddies in hand... cold again tonight.
