Catching Up at Home

Short blog today, since I spend the entire day here catching up on all sorts of things: did the laundry, wrote some letters, organized some cards to be mailed out, finished the last of the unpacking upstairs, put away all the goodies from our trip (cans of favourite custard for the children, new fun toys), wrote a few emails, watered my lovely flowers at the front door, read through some magazines Gail had passed on, enjoyed my very late breakfast of Acme sourdough bread with egg, "bacon", and two cups of hot tea while reading the paper, chatted with Susan and Finn on the phone, and let Doug go off to a double feature on his own so that I could do all of the above. Took a break later on for one of my empire biscuits from Judi and my afternoon tea. Even though it looks different from the ones we used to get, it tasted every bit as good as they used to. Yum!

I got lots done but somehow it felt like a long lazy day, and I enjoyed it all. The house looks so good and I am inspired to continue going through all the rest of the little sorting jobs that need tending to.
