Vacaville

Doug and I used to go to the outlet mall in Vacaville quite frequently, in the years we were buying clothes for the children here and in Toronto. But for the past couple of years we have gone perhaps twice a year, and often when visitors are here. But I needed some footed sleeper blankets for Mirei and a few extra little gifts to take back east, so today was the day for that outing.

The last time we were there, in early November, Doug had his vestibular "accident" and we didn't do any shopping at all. Walking into Fresh Choice for lunch, I know that was on both our minds. Doug commented on the way out after lunch, that the last time he had to be helped to the car by two strong men. He knows he has come a long way since then, but is still anxious to be feeling the way he was two months ago!

We both enjoyed our big healthy lunches today; I couldn't decide between the always delicious butternut squash soup and the corn with green chili soup, so had both. We both enjoyed the roasted fingerling potatoes, and I had them on a side dish, along with a little mac' n' cheese, a small slice of pizza, and my favourite muffin of all ... a corn muffin. That completed my carbs portion.

After that we were well fueled up for browsing among the various sections of the malls. I got everything on my list except for stocking up on birthday/anniversary cards; there used to be a wonderful card and paper shop, but now there is only a very small outlet store with very little selection.

We stopped in at both Costco and at Trader Joe's on the way home, and then enjoyed a long quiet evening here, with a break to watch Billy Connolly touring Australia, always rollicking good fun.

Oh yes, after those hearty lunches we had a bowl of fresh fruit and yogurt for our dinner.
