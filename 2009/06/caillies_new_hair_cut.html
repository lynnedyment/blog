Caillie's New Hair Cut!

Kelly surprised us with this photo of Caillie today, and it caught us all by surprise ... Doug, me, my sisters, and friends. What a difference bangs have made in her look! Caillie has always had the most beautiful hair, thick, with lots of natural highlights, and she often puts it up with ribbons or ties, or looped up in various styles. It always looked lovely. But, the bangs she had cut today just make our "little" girl look like a maturing young lady! I kept looking and looking at the photo, over and over today, not quite believing that such a small change could make a big difference. I think Caillie has been quite taken aback by all the attention this cut has given her, but she'll get used to it, I'm sure!
