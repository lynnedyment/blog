Riku and I Make a Carrot Cake

Riku was keen to get busy making our cake after his day away from home, so we put on our aprons and got to work! I took lots of photos of him, but here are just six, showing some of the stages of our baking: cracking eggs, sifting, mixing, pouring, tasting, and spreading. We always add coconut and pineapple to our carrot cake too, and Riku can hardly wait to taste it tomorrow. We couldn't ice it tonight as we are out of icing sugar. Just as well, as there was no time in our busy evening!
