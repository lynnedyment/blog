Whipping Through Those Emails!

This was a very productive day for me. I had nothing planned for the entire day but to get as many emails answered as I could, touching base with so many friends I have neglected for so long. I wrote emails to Kei in Japan, Judith in Vancouver, Gary Peters in Hamilton, Sheila (one of my doula moms), Tay (now living in Idaho), and Liz in Toronto, as well as many, many others. Seeing those numbers go down just felt so good, and I look forward to hearing back from them all soon. I'm now beginning to see their is a light at the end of the inbox tunnel! I just have to keep writing!

I wrote to Bryn and asked him to cancel my Monday flight, feeling so sad that I won't be seeing Riku turn seven, but also knowing it will be good not to have to wait nine months between visits with him. This would have been the case if I had seen him in December, as I don't expect him here until next August. Mirei and Bryn plan to be here in the spring.

Doug and I ate our dinner upstairs watching a movie, a favourite treat for us ... feels quite decadent, I must say!

I have pulled some photos from years past, since I don't have any from today to post. Enjoy these pics of Riku on this same date in 2004, Amelia in 2005, Amelia and Riku at the playground in 2006, Finn in 2007, and a beautiful tree in full autumn foliage in Niagara Falls in 2008.
